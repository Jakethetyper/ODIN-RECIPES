Tuna Salad

Tuna Salad Dish

This recipe is one of my favorites! It's easy,
healthy, cheap, and can be used in so many different ways!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cans of Tuna
  • 1/4 Cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 Stalk of Celery
  • 2 Tablespoons of Red Onion
  • 1-2 Tablespoons of chopped parsley
  • 1-2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Drain the liquid from the tuna cans
  2. Add the tuna, mayonnaise, diced celery, diced red onions, herbs , dijon mustard, and salt and pepper to a mixing bowl
  3. Stir all the ingredients until well combined
  4. Enjoy it out of the bowl, wrapped in lettuce, or in a sandwich!
