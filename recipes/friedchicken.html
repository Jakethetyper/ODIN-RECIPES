Fried Chicken

Air Fried Chicken

when you're feeling lazy and want something done quick with great taste
come make some Fried Chicken with your air fryer!

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lbs of Chicken
  • 2 Cups Buttermilk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups of Flour
  • 1 Tablespoon of Cajun Seasoning
  • 1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil Spray

Steps

  1. Salt and Pepper chicken thighs and legs
  2. In a medium sized bowl add the buttermilk and eggs and whisk, in another bowl add the flour, cajun, garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper
  3. Dredge the chicken in the flour, then the buttermilk, and then in the flour again
  4. Place in the air fryer
  5. Cook at 360 for 15-20 minutes, open the basket and spray and flour with the cooking spray. Turn the chicken and cook for another 5-10 minutes until 165 degrees and no longer pink
  6. Your chicken
  7. is served.
