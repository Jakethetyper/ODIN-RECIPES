Dragon Roll

Dragon Roll is another popular sushi roll served at American-style sushi restaurants
and buffet joints. Itâ€™s not traditional sushi you can find in Japan, but I can see why
this imaginative sushi is such a favorite.

Ingredients

  • 8 Pieces of Shrimp Tempura
  • 2 Avocados
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 2 Cups of Sushi Rice
  • 2 Sheets of Nori
  • Spicy Mayo
  • Unagi Sauce

Steps

  1. Gather the Ingredients
  2. Cut the cucumber lengthwise into quarters. Remove the seeds and then again cut in half lengthwise into thin strips. You will have 8 strips.
  3. Cut the avocado, remove the skin and thinly slice the avocado
  4. Line the bamboo mat with plastic wrap and place the nori sheet on top, shiny side down
  5. Dip your hands in vinegar water and spread sushi rice over the nori
  6. Flip the nori sheet over and place two pieces of the shrimp tempura, two cucumber strips and some tobiko and the bottom end
  7. From the botom end, start rolling the nori sheet over the filling tightly and firmly with the bamboo mat until the bottom edge reaches the nori sheet.
  8. Place the bamboo mat over the top and tightly squeeze the roll
  9. Remove the bamboo mat, and then place the avocado slices on top if the roll
  10. Layer the roll with plastic wrap again, and then cut the roll into 1/8s
  11. Put some tobiko on top and layer the top with spicy mayo and unagi sauce
  12. Wallah! You're done, enjoy!
